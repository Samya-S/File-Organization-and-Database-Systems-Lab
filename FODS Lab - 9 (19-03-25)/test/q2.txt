Q2: Job with time stamp 14 writes a variable K at time 47. 
Jobs with time stamp 6, 28 reads K at time 86 and 38 respectively.
What need to exist with what time stamp.
Write answer in the following form:
Write(K) between u and v with time stamp between g and h.
Write smallest possible value of u and g and biggest possible for v and h.

Solution:
48-85 with time stamp less than 6.
0-37 with Time stamp 15-27
87-inf with TS bigger than 28
